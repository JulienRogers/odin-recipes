Healthier Homemade Hamburger Helper

This recipe from Half Baked Harvest is a favourite and a top recommendation of mine to friends and family. Swap out the boxed stuff and make everyone's childhood dinner completely homemade, and healthier too. It is delicious and so easy to make! Seriously, there is almost no prep and it only takes 30 minutes to make. It's made with ground beef, seasonings, pasta and a little shredded zuchhni. Feel free to add your other fav veggies to make this recipe your own. Comfort food at its best.

Right to the ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • sea salt and black pepper
  • 1tsp chili powder
  • 1tsp paprika
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated or minced
  • 1lb short cut pasta
  • 1 medium zucchini, grated
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • fresh parsley, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot set over medium hight heat, add the olive oil, onion and beef. Season with salt and pepper. Cook until brown all over, breaking up the meat as you go, about 10 minutes. Stir in the chili powder, paprika, and garlic powder, cook another minute.
  2. Add the pasta and zucchini and toss to coat, then pour in the broth and milk. Add the ketchup and stir to combine. Season with salt.
  3. Bring to a gentle boil over medium high heat. Simmer 5-8 minutes until the pasta is al dente, stirring often.
  4. Stir in the cheese and cook another few minutes until very creamy.
  5. Divide the pasta among bowls and top with fresh parsley and your favourite hot sauce. Enjoy!
