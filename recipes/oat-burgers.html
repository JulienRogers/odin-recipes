Oat Burgers - The BEST no meat burger

Ricardo Cuisine Mary-Joanne Boucher's Oat Burgers

The perfect test substitute for beef burgers and a great weeknight dinner. This recipe is easy to memorize and can become a staple in your household. Juicy, full of flavour and filling. On top of this it is filled with protein and complex carbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4cup sesame or flax seeds
  • 1/4cup sunflower or pumpkin seeds
  • 2tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2tbsp dry parsley
  • 1/2cup water
  • 2tbsp tamari or soy sauce
  • sea salt
  • 1tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the oatmeal, breadcrumbs, grated cheese, eggs, sesame and sunflower seeds, parsley, pepper, paprika, and vegetable flakes. Add the water and mix well until smooth. Leave to rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Shape into 8 patties. Careful, it is a bit sticky! Keep your hands wet to help keep the mixture from sticking to your hands
  3. Heat olive oil in a large heavy skillet and fry the patties until golden brown.
  4. Pour enough water to cover halfway up the patties, add the tamari and cook over medium heat for 10 minutes, turning them a few times in the cooking juices.
  5. When the liquid is completely reduced, serve immediately.

Impress your friends, this will be the best tasting veggie burger they've ever had.
